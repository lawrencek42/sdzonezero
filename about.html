Why San Diego Zone Zero Exists

My name is Lawrence, and I live in University City — one of many San Diego communities located in a Very High Fire Hazard Severity Zone.

When devastating fires tore through Los Angeles communities in 2025, it became clear how quickly fire risk can escalate in California neighborhoods like ours. Soon after, San Diego adopted the new Zone Zero wildfire defensible space ordinance.

More than one million homeowners in the county are now required to make changes to their properties before February 2027. Yet most residents have never heard of Zone Zero — and the official guidance is scattered across government websites and technical documents.

San Diego Zone Zero was created to simplify the process, educate homeowners, and make compliance achievable before the deadline.

What We Do

San Diego Zone Zero Solutions is a San Diego-based wildfire compliance education company. We translate complex government regulations into clear, actionable steps homeowners can follow.

We work with homeowners, HOAs, rental property owners, and property managers to evaluate properties, identify compliance risks, and connect residents with trusted local contractors who can complete Zone Zero upgrades.

Our goal is to help homeowners protect their homes, maintain insurance eligibility, and avoid last-minute contractor shortages before the deadline.
